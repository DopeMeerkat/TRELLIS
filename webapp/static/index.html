Image to Mesh

Fast, lightweight image-to-mesh generation built for rapid iteration.

How it works

Generate a batch quickly, choose the strongest mesh, then refine it in higher quality.

1

Pick an image

Use a clear product or object photo (JPG, PNG, or WEBP). A clean subject helps produce stronger geometry.

2

Generate a fast batch

Open Variant Exploration and generate up to 4 candidates. Each mesh is lightweight and usually takes around 20 seconds.

3

Choose the best candidate

Inspect variants in the viewer or multiview mode, then click Use this on the best mesh to open the shared result page.

4

Refine in higher quality

From the result page, run Regenerate in Higher Quality to keep the same seed and source image while improving detail.
